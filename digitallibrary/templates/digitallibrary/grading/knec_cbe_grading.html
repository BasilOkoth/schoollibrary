Key Principles

  • No more mean scores - per subject reporting
  • Descriptive levels (EE, ME, AE, BE)
  • Focus on competency mastery
  • Placement based on subject performance

Placement Tracks

  • EE (Exceeding): National Schools
  • ME (Meeting): Extra-County Schools
  • AE (Approaching): County Schools
  • BE (Below): Sub-County Schools

Important Notes

  • No A, B, C, D, E grades
  • EE is highest, BE is lowest
  • Individual subject placement
